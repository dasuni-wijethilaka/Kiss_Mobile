About Us

Founded in 2023, Kiss Mobile Pvt Ltd is a dynamic and rapidly growing private limited company in Sri Lanka, incorporated under the Companies Act No. 07 of 2007.


We specialize in importing and wholesaling high-quality pre-owned Apple devices, selected premium smartphones, brand-new keypad phones, and superior mobile accessories sourced directly from Hong Kong and Thailand.


Our operations are driven by strategic-level management under the guidance of highly experienced marketing professionals, ensuring a perfect blend of innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.


As one of the fastest-growing companies in the mobile industry, Kiss Mobile Pvt Ltd is deeply committed to Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), continuously working toward sustainable growth built on trust, integrity, and community engagement.


We stand out in the market by offering our partners to inimitable dealer service,minimizing product returns,fast and reliable supply chain, ensuring repeat sales from dealers and create values for end-users,s satisfaction as marketing-driven professional team


At Kiss Mobile Pvt Ltd, we don’t just sell devices — we build lasting connections and empower your business growth.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most differentiated and trusted B2B partner in the mobile phone and accessories industry by delivering premium international products, unparalleled service, and innovative strategies that redefine wholesaling.

Our Mission

Import and distribute high-quality iPhones and mobile accessories from global markets, primarily Hong Kong and Thailand to Sri Lankan businesses with a commitment to excellence, reliability, and integrity. We strive to offer unique customer experiences through unmatched service, forward-thinking strategies, and a relentless focus on building long-term, value-driven partnerships that set us apart in the wholesale landscape.
